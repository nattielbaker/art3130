Our Motto

I don’t own the road, I share it and available space at every opportunity.

drivefriendly.info is born from a realization that we are a community of drivers that must operate in unison, and to recognize that doing so in a genuinely friendly manner is far superior to the alternative.

Our Drive

Reinvigorate the joy of driving, daily

Restore civility to interactions, with fellow drivers

Reduce stress

Realize a community of drivers sharing something they never owned anyway

I Aspire to...

Student

kindly give way, politely move over, encourage a peaceful merge, have empathy for others in a hurry (you’ve been there before), recognize posted speed limits, very sparingly use horns for alerts – not as an extension of an appendage, express your intelligence by being tolerant of different driving skill levels (new drivers, aged, distracted), communicate intentions and warnings with turn signals, brake lights and 4-way flashers, revere your individual responsibility directing the mass and inertia of your vehicle and potential infliction of severe harm it can cause to you, others, your loved ones, passengers, friends, lovers, neighbors, children of your friends, lovers, neighbors, and complete strangers who are just like you

Apprentice

foresee opportunities to increase someone else’s joy of driving, share the message of friendly driving

Teacher

genuinely smile and feel peace in your heart while acting as above

By Signing this petition I agree to...

1. Strive to never drive aggressively

2. Be patient, especially in parking lots and traffic delays

3. To look for opportunities to express kindness while driving

4. And especially to smile often and enjoy driving with others
